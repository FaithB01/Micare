Our Story

Established in 2015, MiCare Fashion House is an independent luxury accessory label that breaks conventions in the world of handbag design.

Taking inspiration from timeless elegance and contemporary style, MiCare fuses design, function, and fashion to produce a collection of beautiful, yet durable handbags and accessories.

Our collection has been personally curated for smart, confident women who are looking for timeless and luxurious designs of superior quality that complement their sophisticated lifestyle.
